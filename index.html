// about me

Who I Am

Hey! I'm Josh, a Game Programmer and Designer from New Jersey, USA. I've been playing games for as long as I can remember, always curious about how they worked behind the scenes.

That curiosity led me to earn a Conservatory Certificate in Fine Arts, a Bachelor's in Digital Cinematography, and later a Master's in Game Design from Full Sail University.

During my studies I interned at Zygobots as Lead Intern, where I contributed to Shattered Melodies — a full action RPG that Ayo Games Studio published on Steam after my graduation. Since then I've continued collaborating with Ayo Games on Little Red Hunter as Lead Programmer, while also building and shipping my own games independently.

What excites me most is figuring out how gameplay systems tick — and then finding ways to make them more efficient, polished, and fun.
